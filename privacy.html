Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we handle your sensitive medical information and your rights under global health data laws.

What Data We Collect

To provide accurate medical services, we collect and store:

Full Name Medical History Lab Results Prescriptions Billing Address
How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for healthcare purposes. We do not sell your personal or medical information to third-party marketers. Usage includes:

  • Sharing results with your assigned doctor.
  • Sending appointment reminders.
  • Processing medical bill payments.
Your Rights

Under HIPAA and GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your medical records.
  • Ask for corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and associated data (where legally permitted).
Security Summary

All medical records are encrypted using AES-256 bit encryption. Your data is stored on HIPAA-compliant servers with 24/7 monitoring.
